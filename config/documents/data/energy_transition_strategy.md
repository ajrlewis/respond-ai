# Energy Transition Strategy

## Strategy Objective

The energy transition strategy targets assets and businesses that support decarbonisation, grid resilience, and efficient energy infrastructure.

## Typical Areas of Focus

- Solar generation assets
- Battery energy storage systems
- Supporting infrastructure for energy networks
- Opportunities linked to system flexibility and reliability

## Underwriting Principles

Core underwriting considerations include:

- Asset quality and technical performance
- Revenue durability and counterparty quality
- Regulatory and policy context
- Route to operational improvement and value creation

## Value Creation Levers

- Active performance management and optimisation
- Contract and revenue profile management
- Capital structure efficiency and refinancing optionality
- Portfolio-level risk diversification

## Key Risk Areas

- Energy price and market volatility
- Regulatory or subsidy framework changes
- Construction/operational delivery risk
- Counterparty and concentration risk

## Risk Mitigation

Portfolio construction, contract quality analysis, downside stress testing, and active monitoring are used to manage these risks over the investment horizon.

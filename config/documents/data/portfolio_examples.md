# Portfolio Case Themes (Illustrative)

This document provides illustrative case themes aligned to Gresham House strategy areas for drafting and retrieval testing.

## Case Theme 1: Distributed Renewable Generation

A portfolio of distributed renewable assets is assessed for opportunities to improve operational uptime, contract profile quality, and long-term resilience of cash flows.

Potential value-creation themes:

- Monitoring and maintenance optimisation
- Counterparty and contract strengthening
- Portfolio-level financing efficiency

## Case Theme 2: Grid Flexibility and Storage

Battery and flexibility-linked assets are evaluated for ability to support energy system balancing and capture diversified revenue streams.

Potential diligence themes:

- Technology and degradation assumptions
- Route-to-market strategy and volatility management
- Regulatory and market participation rules

## Case Theme 3: Multi-Asset Diversification

Portfolio construction is used to reduce concentration risk by balancing technology, geography, and revenue profile across assets.

Potential risk controls:

- Concentration limits
- Scenario and sensitivity testing
- Periodic strategy re-underwriting

# Prior RFP-Style Answer Library (Drafting Reference)

## Q: How would you describe your investment approach?

Gresham House applies a specialist alternative investment approach centered on long-term themes, disciplined underwriting, and active asset management. The process is designed to balance return objectives with rigorous governance, risk control, and stewardship.

## Q: How are ESG factors integrated in practice?

ESG is integrated from pre-screening through due diligence, committee decision-making, and portfolio monitoring. Material environmental, social, and governance factors are assessed alongside financial considerations, and progress is monitored through regular reporting.

## Q: How do you manage risk in energy transition investing?

Risk management combines conservative underwriting, diversification, and active portfolio oversight. Key risks such as policy change, operational performance, and market volatility are reviewed at investment and throughout ownership.

## Q: What is your governance model for investment decisions?

Transactions are subject to formal governance review with documented assumptions, challenge, and approval pathways. Investment, risk, and compliance functions work together to ensure decisions are evidence-based and aligned to mandate requirements.

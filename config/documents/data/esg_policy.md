# Responsible Investment and ESG Framework

## Policy Summary

Gresham House integrates environmental, social, and governance considerations into investment selection, ownership, and reporting.

## Lifecycle Integration

ESG is considered at each stage:

1. Pre-screening and exclusion checks
2. Due diligence and investment recommendation
3. Investment committee review
4. Ongoing portfolio monitoring and engagement
5. Exit planning and lessons learned

## Environmental Topics

Common focus topics include decarbonisation pathways, resource efficiency, resilience, biodiversity, and climate-related risk management.

## Social Topics

Common focus topics include health and safety, workforce practices, community impact, and stakeholder engagement.

## Governance Topics

Common focus topics include board effectiveness, control environment, conflicts management, and quality of disclosures.

## Reporting Approach

Portfolio-level and asset-level metrics are monitored and communicated to clients through periodic reporting packs, with narrative context for progress and limitations.

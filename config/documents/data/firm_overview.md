# Gresham House Firm Overview

## Positioning

Gresham House is a specialist alternative asset manager focused on delivering financial returns by investing in themes shaping the future economy.

## Strategy Platforms

The firm invests across real assets and strategic equity, with core focus areas including:

- Natural capital
- Energy transition
- Sustainable infrastructure
- Strategic equity
- Social impact and growth-oriented opportunities

## Client Focus

The platform is designed for long-term investors seeking diversified exposure to alternative assets, with an emphasis on durability of cash flows, active stewardship, and transparency.

## Investment Approach

Gresham House applies a disciplined, research-led investment process:

1. Thematic and sector-focused sourcing
2. Structured due diligence on commercial, technical, legal, and ESG factors
3. Investment committee decision-making with defined governance gates
4. Active asset management after deployment

## Responsible Investment Lens

Investment decisions are aligned to responsible investment principles, with ESG integration and monitoring embedded through the investment lifecycle.

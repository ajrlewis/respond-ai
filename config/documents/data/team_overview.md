# Team & Operating Model

## Team Structure

Gresham House operates with strategy-aligned investment teams supported by central risk, legal, finance, and compliance functions.

## Core Capabilities

- Alternative asset underwriting across real assets and strategic equity
- Operational asset management and portfolio monitoring
- ESG and sustainability integration in diligence and ownership
- Investor reporting and governance oversight

## Decision Governance

Investment proposals are reviewed through formal governance forums, including investment committee review and challenge. Material risks, assumptions, and downside cases are documented before approval.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration

Portfolio teams work with specialists in sustainability, risk, and legal/compliance to ensure consistent standards from initial screening through asset management and exit.

## Monitoring and Escalation

Post-investment performance, risk indicators, and compliance obligations are tracked through recurring review cycles, with defined escalation pathways for exceptions.

# Firm Overview

Gresham House is a specialist alternative asset manager focused on sustainable investment strategies across real assets and strategic equity.

The firm manages approximately £9–10 billion in assets on behalf of a diverse investor base including pension funds, family offices, endowments, charities, and wealth managers.

## Core Strategies

- Forestry
- Energy Transition (including solar, battery storage, and infrastructure)
- Sustainable Infrastructure
- Strategic Equity

## Investment Philosophy

Gresham House aims to deliver attractive risk-adjusted returns through long-term investment in real assets that benefit from structural growth trends, particularly those linked to sustainability and decarbonisation.

The firm emphasises:
- Capital preservation
- Long-term income generation
- Responsible investment practices

## Geographic Focus

Primary focus is on:
- United Kingdom
- Europe

With selective expansion into global opportunities aligned with its strategy.

## Differentiation

- Deep sector specialisation in sustainable real assets
- Integrated ESG and impact framework
- Long-term investment horizon
- Active asset management approach

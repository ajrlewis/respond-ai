# Portfolio Examples – Energy Transition

## UK Solar Portfolio (Distributed Solar)

Gresham House manages a diversified portfolio of distributed solar PV assets across the UK.

- Capacity: ~150 MW
- Asset type: Rooftop and ground-mounted solar
- Revenue model: Combination of feed-in tariffs and power purchase agreements (PPAs)

### Value Creation

- Portfolio aggregation to achieve scale
- Operational optimisation through improved asset monitoring
- Refinancing to reduce cost of capital

## Grid-Scale Battery Storage Project

Investment in a UK-based battery storage project supporting grid stability.

- Capacity: 50 MW
- Revenue streams: Frequency response services and capacity market

### Strategic Rationale

- Supports renewable energy integration
- Benefits from increasing demand for grid flexibility

---

## Co-located Solar + Storage Project

A hybrid renewable energy project combining solar generation with battery storage.

- Improves energy dispatch flexibility
- Enhances revenue stability through multiple income streams

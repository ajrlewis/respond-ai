# Prior RFP Answers

## Q: What differentiates your investment approach?

Our approach is differentiated by our focus on sustainable real assets, deep sector expertise, and active asset management capabilities. We combine long-term investment horizons with rigorous ESG integration, allowing us to generate stable income and capital growth while contributing to environmental objectives.

---

## Q: How do you manage ESG risks?

We integrate ESG considerations throughout the investment lifecycle, from screening and due diligence through to ongoing asset management. Dedicated ESG frameworks and metrics ensure risks are identified, monitored, and mitigated effectively.

---

## Q: Describe your renewable energy experience.

We have extensive experience investing in renewable energy infrastructure, including solar PV and battery storage. Our portfolio includes distributed solar assets and grid-scale storage projects, with a focus on long-term contracted revenues and operational optimisation.

# Regulatory & Policy Risk Management

## Overview

Investments in renewable energy infrastructure are subject to evolving regulatory and policy environments.

## Key Risks

- Changes to subsidy regimes
- Energy market reforms
- Planning and permitting challenges

## Mitigation Strategies

- Focus on markets with stable regulatory frameworks
- Diversification across geographies
- Structuring investments with conservative assumptions
- Monitoring policy developments closely

## Governance

Dedicated internal teams track regulatory developments and incorporate them into investment decision-making and asset management processes.

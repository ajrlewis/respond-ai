# Investment Process

## 1. Origination

Opportunities are sourced through:
- Proprietary networks
- Industry relationships
- Direct partnerships

## 2. Screening

Initial screening assesses:
- Strategic fit
- Risk-return profile
- ESG considerations

## 3. Due Diligence

Comprehensive due diligence includes:
- Technical analysis
- Financial modelling
- Legal and regulatory review
- ESG assessment

## 4. Investment Committee

All investments are reviewed and approved by an internal Investment Committee.

## 5. Execution

Structured transactions with appropriate financing and governance frameworks.

## 6. Asset Management

Post-investment focus includes:
- Performance monitoring
- Operational improvements
- Risk management

## 7. Exit

Exit strategies may include:
- Asset sale
- Portfolio sale
- Refinancing

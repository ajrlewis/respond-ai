# Responsible Investment & ESG Policy

## Overview

Gresham House integrates environmental, social, and governance (ESG) considerations into all stages of the investment lifecycle.

## ESG Integration

ESG factors are assessed during:
- Initial screening
- Due diligence
- Investment decision-making
- Ongoing asset management

## Environmental Focus

Key environmental priorities include:
- Carbon reduction and decarbonisation
- Renewable energy generation
- Biodiversity protection (particularly in forestry investments)

## Social Considerations

- Community engagement
- Health and safety standards
- Stakeholder impact assessments

## Governance

- Strong oversight and reporting frameworks
- Alignment of management incentives
- Transparent reporting to investors

## Monitoring & Reporting

The firm tracks key ESG metrics across its portfolio, including:
- Carbon intensity
- Energy generation
- Environmental impact indicators

Regular ESG reporting is provided to investors.

## Compliance

Gresham House aligns with relevant regulatory frameworks and industry standards, including:
- UK sustainability disclosure requirements
- EU SFDR (where applicable)
